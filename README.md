## About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate researching **cryptography**, **zero-knowledge proofs**, **privacy-preserving protocols**, **blockchain systems**, and **agentic security**.

My current interests include:

- Zero-knowledge proof systems and zkSNARKs
- Polynomial commitments and proof-system engineering
- Privacy-preserving smart contracts and compliance protocols
- Threshold encryption and MEV-resistant cryptographic protocols
- Agent authorization, verifiable credentials, and policy proofs
- AI-assisted cryptographic proof writing and review
